Riviera is a sophisticated and elegant restaurant located in the heart of the city. It offers a seamless blend of modern cuisine and classic favorites, all served in a stylish and inviting atmosphere. With a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients and innovative culinary techniques, Riviera is renowned for its delectable dishes, exceptional service, and an extensive selection of fine wines and cocktails. The restaurant's ambiance is a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and warm hospitality, providing the perfect setting for intimate dinners, social gatherings, and special celebrations. Whether it's a romantic evening for two or a lively group event, Riviera promises an unforgettable dining experience.

OUR MENUS

Riviera is where culinary innovation meets the timeless charm of breakfast and lunch classics. Our menu, a treasure trove of gourmet pancakes, artisanal breakfast cocktails, and the best mimosas in Denver, promises an unrivaled dining experience. Savor the flavors of Denver from decadent French toast to savory benedicts, each dish at Riviera is crafted to perfection, making us the go-to spot for weekend brunch in Denver. Whether you're indulging in a family-friendly brunch or seeking the perfect brunch spot for a lazy Sunday, Riviera's unique menu caters to all.

Private Dining and Events

Riviera is a warm and inviting space perfect for your next gathering. Our dining room seats up to 65 guests and is ideal for birthdays, showers, and special occasions.
