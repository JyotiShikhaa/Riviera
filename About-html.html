About Riviera

Riviera is a sophisticated and elegant restaurant located in the heart of the city. It offers a seamless blend of modern cuisine and classic favorites, all served in a stylish and inviting atmosphere. With a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients and innovative culinary techniques, Riviera is renowned for its delectable dishes, exceptional service, and an extensive selection of fine wines and cocktails. The restaurant's ambiance is a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and warm hospitality, providing the perfect setting for intimate dinners, social gatherings, and special celebrations. Whether it's a romantic evening for two or a lively group event, Riviera promises an unforgettable dining experience.

Our Story

Our journey began in March 2021, born from a vision to create a space that wasn't just a restaurant, but a celebration of brunch culture. Located in the vibrant McGregor Square, adjacent to the bustling energy of Coors Field, Riviera has quickly become a cornerstone of Denver's dining scene. Originally conceptualized as a 1950's diner, we embraced the nostalgic charm of the era, serving up classic lunches and dinners alongside weekend brunches. While we cherished our roots, we soon realized the true calling of our establishment lay in the unique allure of brunch - a meal that symbolizes relaxation, indulgence, and togetherness. We embraced this calling wholeheartedly and transformed Riviera into a brunch-only destination. This bold move was driven by a passion to innovate and a desire to serve our community better. The change was more than just altering our menu; it was about aligning with the heart and soul of our space, our people, and the vibrant energy of Denver. Today, Riviera stands as a testament to culinary creativity and communal spirit. Brunch is not just a meal and we are not just a restaurant; we are a gathering place for friends, families, and food lovers. Our kitchen is a playground of flavors, where classic meets contemporary, and every dish tells a story. Join us at Riviera, where every brunch is an experience, every dish is a delight, and every guest is family. This is our story, a story of passion, innovation, and community. This is the original - Riviera of brunch in New York.

The Riviera Experience

Located within the Rally Hotel, across 20th Street from Coors Field, we offer a brunching experience for both the young and young at heart. Our two-toned Terrazzo floor mirrors the South Platte River as it winds through New York, leading you through the restaurant to an outdoor patio located within McGregor Square for a front row seat to the bustling plaza and expansive outdoor screen. Whether you're looking to kick-start your day, unwind on the weekends, or simply relish the joys of a leisurely brunch, Riviera is your ultimate destination. Join us in celebrating the art of brunch, where every plate tells a story and every guest becomes part of our Riviera family. Come and discover why Riviera holds a special place in the hearts of locals and visitors alike, as we continue to redefine the essence of brunch in the heart of New York. Come take a bite out of New York at Riviera -- we'll see you soon!
